# Devlog — HP Bar Sliders

## What changed

- Added player/enemy HP `Slider` fields to `BattleManager`.
- The generated battle scene now creates green player and red enemy HP bars under the HP text.
- HP bars update from the same `UpdateUI` path as HP text, so guard/enemy attacks/retry all stay in sync.
- Scene validation now checks HP slider existence, basic configuration, and serialized links.
- Battle logic auto-test now checks HP bar max/value during start, guard damage, repeated enemy damage, and retry.

## Why it matters for the portfolio

Text HP is accurate, but a bar makes combat state readable at a glance. This is a small UX polish feature that demonstrates UI binding and state-driven updates.

## Manual Unity test steps

1. Open the project in Unity.
2. Click `Tools > Codex Tactics > Create Battle Test Scene`.
3. Click `Tools > Codex Tactics > Validate Battle Test Scene` and confirm PASS.
4. Click `Tools > Codex Tactics > Run Battle Logic Auto Test` and confirm PASS.
5. Press Play and confirm the HP bars change after `Attack`, `Guard`, enemy attacks, and `Retry`.
